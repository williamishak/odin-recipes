How to Make Pizza

Finished product of the recipe

Description of the recipe:

A deliciously cheesy pizza topped with rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and a sprinkle of basil, baked to a crispy, golden perfection.

Ideal for satisfying your cravings with every bite!

Ingredients needed:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps in order:

  1. Preheat the oven. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C), and if using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. Prepare the dough. Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface until it's about 1/4-inch thick. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the sauce. Evenly spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving about 1/2 inch of space around the edges for the crust.
  4. Add cheese and toppings. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by any additional toppings of your choice (like pepperoni, veggies, or garlic).
  5. Drizzle with olive oil. Lightly drizzle olive oil over the pizza to enhance the flavor and help the cheese brown.
  6. Bake the pizza. Slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or place the baking sheet in the oven. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Serve. Let the pizza cool for a minute, slice, and enjoy!
