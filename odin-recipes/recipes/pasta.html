How to Make Lasagna

Finished product of the recipe.

Description of the recipe:

This recipe consists of very simple key steps that include a few ingredients that you can find at your local store.

This recipe does not take much time, and is very convenient to those that like to cook fast.

Ingredients needed:

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Chicken
  • Tomato
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper

Steps in order:

  1. Boil Water. Fill a large pot with water, add a generous amount of salt, and bring it to a rolling boil.
  2. Cook the Pasta. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Prepare the Sauce. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add Tomato. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir. Bring the sauce to a simmer, and add fresh basil, salt, and pepper to taste. Let it cook for about 5-10 minutes to blend the flavors.
  5. Drain the Pasta.Once the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the rest in a colander.
  6. Serve. Plate the pasta, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, and add extra fresh basil or black pepper if desired.
  7. Enjoy.Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade pasta!
