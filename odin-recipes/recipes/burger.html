How to Make a Burger

A finished photo of the recipe

Description of the recipe:

A juicy beef patty grilled to perfection, topped with melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, tomato, and a smear of tangy sauce.

The perfect recipe for a sunny day out; not too time-consuming, yet so tasty.

Ingredients needed:

  • Ground Beef
  • Buns
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Butter

Steps in order:

  1. Season the beef. Form the ground beef into patties (about 1/3 lb each) and season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat the pan. Heat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Lightly oil the surface to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook the patties. Place the patties on the grill or pan, cooking for 3-4 minutes on the first side. Flip the patties and cook another 3-4 minutes, or until desired doneness.
  4. Add cheese. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty and cover with a lid to melt the cheese.
  5. Assemble the burger. Spread your favorite sauce (ketchup, mayo, etc.) on the bottom bun. Add the lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onion. Place the cheesy patty on top, then cover with the top bun.
